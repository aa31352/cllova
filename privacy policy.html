This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you interact with our website, products, and services. We comply with global data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable regulations.

By using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

3. What Data We Collect

We collect various types of personal data, including but not limited to:

3.1. Information You Provide to Us

  • Name, email address, phone number, and business details when you register or contact us.
  • Payment and billing details for subscriptions and services.
  • Any content you provide while using our AI and automation tools.

3.2. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage logs.
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, pixels, and tracking tools to improve user experience.

3.3. Third-Party Data Collection

We may receive data from third-party platforms such as Google Analytics, payment processors, and marketing tools.

4. How We Use Your Data

We process your data to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Customize your experience and offer personalized recommendations.
  • Send promotional offers, newsletters, and updates (you can opt out anytime).

  • Protect against fraud, cyber threats, and unauthorized access.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

We process personal data based on:

  • Consent: When you provide permission for marketing or data collection.
  • Contractual Obligation: When data processing is necessary to deliver our services.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law (e.g., tax reporting, law enforcement requests).
  • Legitimate Interest: For security, analytics, and business improvements.

We do not sell your data. However, we may share it with trusted third parties, such as:
